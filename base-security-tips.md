# Base Security Tips

## Protect Your Wallet

Never share your private key or recovery phrase with anyone.

## Verify Websites

Always double-check website URLs before connecting your wallet.

## Use Trusted Applications

Interact with reputable projects and research them before using their services.

## Beware of Scams

Be cautious of messages, emails, or social media posts promising free rewards or guaranteed profits.

## Keep Software Updated

Update your wallet and device software regularly to benefit from the latest security improvements.

## Conclusion

Good security practices help users safely explore the Base ecosystem and reduce potential risks.
